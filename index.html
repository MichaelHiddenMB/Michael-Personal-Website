My Story

Michael McCarroll was born on February 18th 2007 in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. He grew there with his mom, dad, and younger brother. Since a young age, he has been interested in coding, joining his elementary school's Lego robotics team in 5th grade. Ever since then he has worked on his own engineering projects in both hardware and software. In June of 2025, he graduated from Shady Side Academy, and he now attends the University of Maryland, College Park as a computer science major.
